About Us

London's premier podcast and media production studio, dedicated to bringing your ideas to life.

Our Story

We are London's best premier podcast studio. We specialize in providing a professional, creative space for podcasters of all levels. With advance equipment and a dedicated team of audio experts, we're here to help you produce high-quality content that stands out.

Whether you're recording your first episode or your hundredth, our mission is to bring your vision to life with impeccable sound and a seamless experience.

Open 7 days a week, 24 hours. Every booking comes fully equipped with an onsite engineer with years of experience. From pre-production planning to post-production editing — we handle it all.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of podcasts can I record?
All formats: interview-style, solo, panel discussions, narrative storytelling, branded content. Audio-only and video podcasts supported.
Do I need to bring my own equipment?
No. Our studio has broadcast-grade microphones, 4K cameras, lighting, and acoustic treatment. We also offer dry hire if you prefer your own gear.
Can I customise the studio setup?
Absolutely. Furniture, backdrops, branding displays, and lighting are all customisable. Multiple creative sets and looks available.
What are your opening hours?
We are open 7 days a week, 24 hours. Sessions available at all hours with pricing varying by time slot.
Is an engineer included?
Yes. Every creative studio booking comes fully equipped with an onsite engineer with years of experience.
Where are you located?
We're at 16A–17A Windsor Street, Uxbridge, UB8 1AB. Easy access from central London.
How do I book a session?
You can book directly through our online booking system, fill out the contact form on our website, or contact us via email at studio@londonmedialounge.co.uk or phone at 07700 175079.
What is dry hire?
Dry hire means we provide the professional studio space and you bring your own equipment and production team. It's perfect for experienced creators who prefer their own gear. Available Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm at £45/hr.
Do you offer post-production services?
Yes. We offer comprehensive post-production including editing, sound design, music composition, podcast hosting, and distribution. Contact us for a bespoke quote.
